# 3.1 Job Seeker Profile and Applications

## 3.1.1 Description and Priority
This feature encompasses the core end-to-end workflow for job seekers on the platform, allowing them to create and manage their digital identities, upload necessary documents, and formally interact with job postings.
**Priority:** Essential

## 3.1.2 Functional Requirements
The following functional requirements govern the job seeker profile and application features:

* **[SRS01]** Job seekers shall be able to register for a user profile. (Essential)
* **[SRS02]** Job seekers shall be able to add and update their personal details, skills, and work experience in their user profile. (Essential)
* **[SRS03]** Job seekers shall be able to upload a CV/resume to their user profile. (Essential)
* **[SRS04]** Job seekers shall be able to search and apply for job postings. (Essential)
* **[SRS05]** Job seeker shall be able to view their submitted job applications and application statuses. (Essential)
