# 3.2 Recruiter Job Posting and Applicant Processing

## 3.2.1 Description and Priority
This feature enables recruiters to manage their organizational hiring needs by posting job vacancies, reviewing the influx of candidate applications, and shortlisting applicants for further hiring stages.
**Priority:** Essential


## 3.2.2 Functional Requirements
The following functional requirements govern the recruiter's job posting and applicant processing features:

* **[SRS06]** Recruiter shall be able to manage their job postings. (Essential)
* **[SRS07]** Recruiter shall be able to view applicants who have applied to the recruiter's job postings. (Essential)
* **[SRS08]** Recruiter should be able to shortlist applicants for further consideration. (Desirable)
