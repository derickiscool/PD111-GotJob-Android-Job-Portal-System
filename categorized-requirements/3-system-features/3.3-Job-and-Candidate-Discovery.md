# 3.3 Job and Candidate Discovery

## 3.3.1 Description and Priority
This feature powers the matching engine of the platform. It allows both job seekers and recruiters to organically discover relevant opportunities or talent through manual search queries or the system's automated, rule-based recommendation algorithm.
**Priority:** Essential


## 3.3.2 Functional Requirements
The following functional requirements govern the discovery and matching engine:

* **[SRS09]** Job seeker shall be able to discover jobs through search or automated recommendations. Automated recommendations shall be generated based on skill, experience and location (in order). (Essential)
* **[SRS10]** Recruiter shall be able to discover candidates through search or automated recommendations. Automated recommendations shall be generated based on skill, experience and location (in order), and shall exclude candidates with incomplete profiles. (Essential)
